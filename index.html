Our Services

Balloon Deliveries

We deliver balloon arrangements and displays locally for a small delivery charge. Surprise someone by ordering a balloon bouquet to be delivered to their home ...or work! In literally any theme, it is guaranteed to put a smile on their face! Alternatively, for more distant addresses, we can send a Balloon-in-a-Box anywhere in the country. Any round foil balloon will pop out of a tissue-lined box containing party poppers, streamers and bubbles, all sealed with colour-matched ribbons and bow. What better way to show someone you're thinking of them!

Weddings

We can transform any venue to look like you'd always dreamt it would be! Tell us your ideas, colour scheme and theme and we'll show you our ideas. Whether ...your budget is small or large, we have something for everyone.

Corporate

We have been helping to create WOW events for corporate customers for many years, from product launches to instore promotions, to work ...Christmas parties. Please contact us to discuss your ideas. Clients include ASDA, Mercedes-Benz, Domino's Pizza, Phones4U, Iceland, Specsavers, Channels Golf Club, Tamdown, M&G and many more.

Our Shop

Balloons & Party stock thousands of lines across our departments. A wider range of partyware including banners, decorations, tableware, ...invitations, table centrepieces, badges, catering supplies and much more. Our growing cake decorating department stocks consumables such as sugarpaste, edible glitters and colourings, as well as sugar flowers, cake toppers and equipment like cupcake cases and boxes, piping bags and nozzles, cake boards and boxes and more! We also hire cake tins in various shapes and sizes and stock many different candles. Our range of gifts include jewellery, frames, age gifts, Me to You products, baby gifts as well as Christening and Wedding gifts. In our greetings card department we have over 6000 cards to choose from, so surely something for everyone! We stock the full Me to You and My Blue Nose Friends plush ranges and have a website dedicated to these products, www.metoyoudirect.co.uk.

Handmade Cakes
